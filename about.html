Our Story

From a single tree in a backyard to a flourishing organic orchard in Buea.

The Inspiration

My love for soursop began when I tried to buy good fruits from the market but could not find fruits that had the true flavor of the fruit. This inspired me to grow my first soursop tree in my yard.

I then took the time to learn how to properly grow healthy and pest-free soursop trees. Through this experience I discovered something very important: When fruits are harvested only when they are fully mature and properly ripened on the tree, they develop a special flavor that is very different from what is commonly found in markets.

Growing the Dream

Most fruits sold in markets are harvested before they are fully ready, which prevents them from developing their full natural flavor.

After enjoying the fruits from my home tree for about four years, a thought came to me: Why not grow this fruit on a larger scale so that more people could have the opportunity to enjoy the real flavor of this wonderful fruit?

In 2020 I decided to start a nursery using seedlings from my original mother tree. In April 2021, I planted my orchard of 550 soursop trees.

Innovation & Quality

Since then many people have had the opportunity to enjoy the real flavor of properly grown soursop fruit.

Another important aspect of this fruit is its perishability. Because fresh soursop does not last long after harvest, we began producing and conserving fresh soursop pulp so people can enjoy it in juices, smoothies and fruit cocktails.

Today one of our main products at L&N Farms is pure organic soursop pulp. We also carefully prepare and dehydrate soursop leaves which are used to make excellent soursop herbal tea.

Experience the Real Flavor

Our orchard is completely organic and all our products reflect our dedication to natural farming and quality fruit.
