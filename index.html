Naturally Grown in Buea

L&N Farms is a dedicated soursop orchard located in the fertile volcanic soils of Buea. We believe in the power of nature, which is why our 550 soursop trees are grown using strictly organic practices.

By allowing our fruits to fully mature and ripen on the tree, we ensure every harvest carries the authentic, rich flavor that soursop lovers crave.

The Art of Harvesting

Harvesting soursop is a delicate process. We wait until the fruit is perfectly mature on the tree before hand-picking each one. This ensures the highest sugar content and the most vibrant tropical flavor.

Our team carefully inspects every fruit, selecting only the best for our fresh market and processing facility. From the tree to the crate, we handle our soursop with the respect it deserves.

From Orchard to Table

Our commitment to quality extends beyond the orchard. We have invested in modern processing facilities right here in Buea to ensure our soursop pulp and leaves are handled with the highest standards of hygiene and care.

By processing our fruits immediately after harvest, we lock in the fresh flavor and nutritional benefits that make our products stand out.
